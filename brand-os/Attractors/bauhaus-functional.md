## Bauhaus Functional

Form serves function, and in doing so becomes beautiful. The brand is a system, not a style — every element has a reason. The feeling of encountering design that has been thought through completely, where nothing could be added or removed without loss.

## Brands in this attractor
- [[Braun]] (Dieter Rams era)
- [[Vitra]]
- [[muji|Muji]] (product philosophy)
- [[Knoll]]
- [[Herman Miller]]

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Geometric sans-serif as systematic choice — Futura as the original, Akzidenz Grotesk, Helvetica. Type used at fixed sizes from a defined scale. Consistent baseline grid. No expressive variation.
- **Palette:** Primary colors as system — not for decoration, but for categorization. Black, white, gray as base. Red, yellow, blue as functional signals. No hybrid tones.
- **Geometry:** Angular with precision — the geometry of manufactured objects. Circles, squares, triangles as primary forms. Ratio-based composition.
- **Density:** Medium — the grid is felt. Comfortable density that implies systematic thought.
- **Textures:** Brushed steel, laminate, polished plastic, powder-coat. Industrial manufacturing as aesthetic.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Neutral, systematic. The brand explains the reason for every decision, but without defensiveness — simply as information.
- **Phrase length:** Medium — complete thoughts, systematic structure. Often uses numbered lists or comparative analysis.
- **Vocabulary:** Design vocabulary (proportion, function, system, modular, grid), manufacturing verbs (produced, assembled, engineered), reduction language (only, essential, without, single).
- **Examples:** "Ten principles of good design." / "As little design as possible." / "Five components. Unlimited configurations."

## Industry adaptations

### Real estate
Systematic residential development using modular construction. Floor plans diagrammatic and clear. Options system: choose from defined configurations. Furniture by Vitra or Knoll specified by developer.

### HoReCa
Contract furniture brand or systematic catering equipment. The product specification is the communication. Configurator as primary sales tool.

### Fashion
Modular wardrobe system. Garments designed to combine in defined ways. Color palette is an annual decision, not a seasonal one. The system is the collection.

### Tech
Design system product or component library. The brand is a system for building brands. Documentation is the product. API design follows the same principles as visual design.

## When to apply
- Client product is systematic by nature — modular, configurable, scalable
- Audience values coherence and system-thinking over individual expression
- Brand operates in furniture, industrial design, design tooling, or workplace product
- "Designed once, serves forever" is the value proposition
