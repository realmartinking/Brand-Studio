## Constructivist Honesty

The brand shows how it is made. Structure is the aesthetic. The feeling of encountering something that refuses to disguise what it is — and you realize you prefer it this way. Raw, but not careless.

- [[Phaidon]] (architecture books)
- [[SCP Furniture]]

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Grotesque with structure — Helvetica, Univers, Aktiv Grotesk. Used systematically, no decorative variation. Type set on visible grid. Rules and guidelines as visual elements.
- **Palette:** Concrete gray, raw linen, umber, iron, chalk white. Materials as colors. No color for color's sake.
- **Geometry:** Angular and declarative — the geometry comes from function, not design. Exposed joints, visible structure, no cover panels.
- **Density:** Medium — enough to show the structure, not so much the structure is obscured.
- **Textures:** Bare concrete, exposed brick, raw steel, unfinished wood. The material IS the texture.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Declarative, economical. No persuasion — only statement. "This is what it is."
- **Phrase length:** Short to medium. No subordinate clauses. Subject → verb → object.
- **Vocabulary:** Material vocabulary (concrete, load-bearing, structural, raw, exposed), function vocabulary (serves, holds, supports, contains), honest qualification (not to everyone's taste, deliberately rough).
- **Examples:** "The beam is the ceiling." / "Concrete, because it is honest." / "Built to be seen, not hidden."

## Industry adaptations

### Real estate
Architecture-led development where structural elements are features — exposed concrete ceilings, visible services, raw columns. Industrial-to-residential conversion. The original building's bones are the design.

### HoReCa
Restaurant where the kitchen is the dining room — no wall between cooking and eating. Service is functional, not theatrical. Menu on a card, printed plainly.

### Fashion
Technical workwear or utilitarian fashion where the construction details are visible — seams outside, zips exposed, pockets functional. No hidden structure.

### Tech
Developer tool or API-first product. Documentation is the interface. The product does not hide how it works — it rewards those who want to understand.

## When to apply
- Product has structural or process integrity that is worth exposing
- Audience values transparency and distrusts polish
- Brand occupies architectural, industrial design, or functional product space
- "Less but better" is the operating principle
