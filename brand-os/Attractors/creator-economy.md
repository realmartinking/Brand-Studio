## Creator Economy

The brand exists to extend what its users can make. The product is in service of the creator's output, not the creator's identity. The feeling of a tool that understands what you are trying to do before you finish explaining.

## Brands in this attractor
- [[figma|Figma]]
- [[Notion]]
- [[Framer]]
- [[Substack]]
- [[Are.na]]

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Clean geometric sans with character — Inter, Sohne, GT America. Regular to medium weight. Type system that accommodates both product UI and marketing materials coherently.
- **Palette:** Brand color that is distinctively theirs. [[figma|Figma]] purple-spectrum, Notion black, Framer blue. White space as canvas metaphor — the space where the creator works.
- **Geometry:** Flexible and modular — the layout system reflects the tools' modularity. Grid visible, canvas metaphor active.
- **Density:** Medium — enough to show the richness of what the tool enables (user output, templates, examples), not so dense it becomes overwhelming.
- **Textures:** Flat digital with user-generated visual richness. The community's work is the most compelling visual material.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Collaborative, encouraging, peer-level. The brand speaks as a fellow creator, not a tool manufacturer.
- **Phrase length:** Medium — clear enough to explain function, brief enough to not waste time.
- **Vocabulary:** Creation verbs (build, design, write, publish, ship, make), multiplier vocabulary (together, with others, your audience, your community), enabling vocabulary (freedom, power, without limits, yours).
- **Examples:** "Design together, anywhere." / "The tool for building on the internet." / "Your thinking. Published." / "Everything you need to make anything."

## Industry adaptations

### Real estate
Architecture and design tool for property professionals. Collaborative project management. Portfolio publishing platform for architects and interior designers.

### HoReCa
Menu design tool, food photography platform, or hospitality brand builder. Co-creation platform for food and beverage entrepreneurs.

### Fashion
Design tool for fashion brands — pattern making, look book builder, line sheet generator. Community of fashion entrepreneurs sharing templates and resources.

### Tech
Core use case — creative professional tool, collaborative design or publishing platform. The users create, the brand enables.

## When to apply
- Product is a tool used to make other things
- User output is the most compelling brand communication
- Community of creators is both audience and brand ambassador
- Co-creation and collaboration are core product values
