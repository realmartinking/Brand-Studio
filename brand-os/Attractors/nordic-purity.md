## Nordic Purity

Order and lightness as a way of life. The brand communicates: simplicity is not poverty, it is discipline. Comfortable for everyone, yet not cheap — democratic in access, high in standard.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Geometric sans-serif — Futura, Aktiv Grotesk, GT Walsheim. Regular weight, never bold. Text aligned to grid strictly, no orphans.
- **Palette:** Off-white, birch, pale sage, dusty rose, concrete gray. One accent — always muted. Maximum saturation 30%.
- **Geometry:** Rounded but not soft — circle and rectangle coexist. Grid is sacred: 8px or 12px base.
- **Density:** Low, but not extreme — content breathes. 60-70% whitespace. Unlike silent-confidence: you can see what the product is.
- **Textures:** Unbleached cotton, untreated wood, recycled cardboard, matte ceramics. Natural materials without romanticizing.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Calm, matter-of-fact, slightly warm. The brand speaks as a thoughtful neighbor, not a consultant.
- **Phrase length:** Medium — 8-15 words. Complete sentences. No rhetorical questions.
- **Vocabulary:** Functional verbs (holds, fits, lasts, works), material adjectives (natural, honest, considered), everyday life (morning, kitchen, drawer, shelf).
- **Examples:** "A notebook that holds your thoughts without opinion." / "Cotton grown without shortcuts." / "The shelf that becomes part of how you live."

## Industry adaptations

### Real estate
Residential development emphasizing thoughtful layout and natural light. Floor plans front and center. Honest materials: concrete, oak, white render. No staged photography — empty apartment with good light.

### HoReCa
Cafe with three items on the menu, done perfectly. Menu on kraft paper. Chairs that are comfortable for two hours of work. No background music or playlist by local DJ.

### Fashion
Basics brand. One photo: the garment flat on white surface with dimension markings. Colorways shown as swatches. Size guide in centimeters.

### Tech
Productivity app with one function per screen. Settings menu has five items. Onboarding skippable. Light mode by default.

## When to apply
- Client wants to be democratic but not cheap
- Product is for daily use (routine > 0.3)
- Target audience values function over status signaling
- Brand operates in lifestyle, home, or productivity category
