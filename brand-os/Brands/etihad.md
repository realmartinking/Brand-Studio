## Etihad Airways

Founded 2003, national carrier of the United Arab Emirates, headquartered in Abu Dhabi. Positioned as the younger, more experiential rival to Emirates. Landor created the brand identity in 2014 — the "Facets of Abu Dhabi" visual system drawing from geometric Islamic art and the Abu Dhabi landscape. The Residence product (three-room private suite on A380) is the most exclusive commercial aviation product in the world. Etihad represents Abu Dhabi's cultural and economic ambitions as a global hub.

## Why this position

All-few 0.6: Deliberately positioned above mass carriers. The brand hierarchy runs from coach to Business Studio to First Apartments to The Residence — each tier is genuinely differentiated, not marketing fiction.

Distance 0.7: Luxury aviation maintains elevated distance — the service comes to you, the experience is curated, the guest is not expected to participate. The Residence has a butler.

Control-release 0.7: Etihad controls the experience completely — every touchpoint choreographed. But the emotional register is release: arrival, escape, the journey as pleasure.

Temperature 0.6: Warm Arabian hospitality as the service philosophy. "Welcome to Abu Dhabi" — hospitality culture from the host culture.

Serious-playful 0.6: The service is serious (precision, safety, luxury standards) but the brand aspires to delight — the Facets visual identity is rich and celebratory, not austere.

## Attractor difference

Palace-grandeur fits the luxury Arabian heritage positioning and the multi-tiered exclusivity. Key differences: Etihad is more technology-coded (aviation = engineering precision, modern fleet) than typical palace-grandeur. Also warmer (0.6 vs attractor's 0.3) because Arabian hospitality culture is fundamentally warm. Distance at 0.7 aligns with the attractor. The brand is more modern (roots_horizon 0.4) than old-money luxury — it was founded in 2003 and the visual identity celebrates contemporary UAE. Adjacent to [[modern-premium]] for the progressive luxury positioning and [[active-resort]] for the travel and escape dimension.

## Visual code

- **Typography:** Custom elegant sans-serif — "ETIHAD AIRWAYS" in refined, spaced letterforms. Clean and premium without being cold.
  *Source: training-data*
- **Palette:** Haze (warm beige), sand gold, dark chocolate brown, and a soft warm grey. The palette evokes Abu Dhabi landscape — not the aggressive golds of some luxury brands.
  *Source: training-data*
- **Geometry:** The "Facets" system — geometric forms derived from Islamic geometric art patterns. Diamond and tessellation forms. Dynamic and distinctive.
  *Source: training-data / Landor case study*
- **Density:** Medium — rich visual world in premium communications, cleaner in wayfinding and operational contexts.
  *Source: training-data*
- **Textures:** Fabric textures (seat materials), sand, warm light. The brand's texture references Arabian craft heritage.

- **Tone:** Warm hospitality authority. Confident but generous — the host who makes you feel welcome, not the brand that makes you feel lucky.
