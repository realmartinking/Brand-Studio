## Hermès

Founded in 1837 as a harness workshop. The Birkin has a waiting list measured in years. The orange box is trademarked. Hermès is the brand that other luxury brands measure themselves against — the standard of what it means to be truly irreplaceable.

## Why this position

Self-community 0.3: Hermès is profoundly individual — a bag is made for you (or at least felt to be). No crowd imagery. No "join us." You are the collector, the connoisseur.

Control-release 0.2: Maximum control. The supply of Birkins is deliberately constrained — scarcity is policy. Craft standards require 15-25 hours per bag. The artisan's stitch mark is inside every product.

Roots-horizon 0.9: The deepest heritage position of any active luxury brand. Harness-making origins are referenced in every collection. The equestrian motif recurs. 1837 is the first word on every surface.

All-few 0.95: The highest exclusivity score in this brand set. The waiting list, the clienteling, the relationship with the store manager required before you can even be offered a Birkin — exclusivity is the product.

Nature-technology 0.1: Leather, silk, linen, wood. Everything traceable to an animal or a field. The brand refuses synthetic materials as philosophical position.

Rhythm 0.9 / Speed 0.9: The slowest luxury experience in the world. The bag takes months to arrive. The in-store consultation is unhurried. Time is the material.

Temperature 0.8: The orange box, the Hermès tan leather, the warm sienna of calfskin — all warm. Even the Paris store smells warm.

Serious-playful 0.6: Unique for a luxury brand — Hermès allows itself to be witty. The silk scarf collections are often irreverent. The annual theme is sometimes absurdist. The seriousness is absolute, but the brand has pleasure in it.

## Attractor difference

Warm-heritage attractor sits at all_few=0.8, roots=0.9, warm=0.9 — very close. Hermès differs in three ways: exclusivity is pushed further (0.95 vs 0.8), control is tighter (0.2 vs 0.3 — Hermès supply management is unique), and repeatability is higher (0.7 vs 0.3) because a Birkin is an event purchase, not a routine one. The warm-heritage attractor is slightly more artisan/approachable; Hermès operates beyond it into a rarefied altitude.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Herbier — собственный шрифт Hermès, гуманистический гротеск с тонкими засечками. Официальные коммуникации — Plantin. Иллюстрированный мир всегда рядом с текстом.
- **Palette:** Оранжевый «Orange Hermès» (Pantone 021) как главный фирменный цвет планеты. Золото. Тёмно-коричневый коробок. Кремовый шёлк. Цвет как уверенность.
- **Geometry:** Геральдическая — конская упряжь, каретный орнамент. Каждый принт — нарратив. «Carré» — квадрат как священная форма бренда.
- **Density:** Низкая в основных коммуникациях — один предмет, одна история. Высокая внутри принтов — миры в квадрате.
- **Textures:** Седельная кожа box calf, шёлк-атлас 90×90, кашемир, крокодил, конская кожа. Каждый материал — именной.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Игривый аристократ. Умный и никогда тяжёлый. Единственный люксовый бренд с подлинным чувством юмора — лёгкость без легкомыслия.
- **Phrase length:** Короткие, часто неожиданные. Слова-сюрпризы там, где ожидалось клише.
