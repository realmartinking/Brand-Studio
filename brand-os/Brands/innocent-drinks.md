## Innocent Drinks

Founded 1999, now majority-owned by Coca-Cola (since 2013). Innocent is the brand that put personality on a bottle of fruit juice. The "Please Recycle Me" cap, the label copy that reads like a letter from a friend, the annual Fruitstock festival, the hats on bottles at Christmas for Age UK. Innocent proved that warmth and humor are a viable FMCG competitive advantage.

## Why this position

Serious-playful 0.9: The most playful brand in this dataset and among the most playful FMCG brands in history. The humor is never forced — it emerges from a genuine brand character (the founding team wrote all copy personally for the first decade). Bottom-of-bottle jokes, letters-to-the-recycling-plant, hats knitted by elderly volunteers.

Distance 0.1: Intimate as an FMCG brand can be. The "talking to you like a friend" voice started when three founders wrote the copy themselves and never fully institutionalized even at scale.

Temperature 0.2: Maximum warmth — fruit colors (orange, yellow, green), rounded bottle shape, soft illustration style, the Christmas hat campaign. Nothing cool enters the brand system.

All-few 0.2: Supermarket shelves, competitive pricing, available everywhere in the UK and Europe. Genuinely accessible.

Nature-technology 0.2: "Nothing but fruit" is the brand claim. The supply chain is the story — which farms, which countries. The product is nature.

Body-mind 0.7: The product is consumed by the body (taste, nutrition, physical sensation of drinking) and the brand makes this explicit — "a bit of goodness," health as happiness. But the mind engages through the humor.

Repeatability 0.1: Daily consumption — a smoothie at breakfast, a juice with lunch. Maximum routine frequency.

Geometry 0.8: Rounded — the bottle shape, the illustration characters (tiny legs, round bodies), the logotype. Nothing sharp in the brand system.

## Attractor difference

Friendly-helper attractor: playful=0.7, intimate=0.2, fast=0.3, warm=0.4, all=0.2. Innocent pushes further into playful (0.9 vs 0.7) and is warmer (0.2 vs 0.4). The difference from the attractor is the FMCG context — friendly-helper is primarily a software/service archetype. Innocent applies the same character to a physical consumable. Body-mind at 0.7 vs attractor's 0.5 reflects the sensory primacy of consuming a product through taste. The brand is also significantly more nature-coded (0.2 vs 0.4) — "nothing but fruit" is the foundational promise.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Рукописный шрифт как основной — граффити-тепло, никакой корпоративности. Дополнение — округлый гротеск. Весь текст на упаковке написан как будто другом.
- **Palette:** Зелёный трава (фирменный), жёлтый лимон, оранжевый мандарин, красный клубника. Цвета самих фруктов — прямая честность.
- **Geometry:** Мягкие органические формы. Бутылка — как маленький человечек. Волнистые линии, никаких острых углов.
- **Density:** Низкая — много места для текста-шутки на обороте. Читаешь этикетку как письмо.
- **Textures:** Гладкий пластик бутылки стал нежным через графику. Текстура — в тексте, не в материале.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Искренний и немного нелепый. Разговаривает как лучший друг который делает тебе хорошее и знает что это забавно.
- **Phrase length:** Короткие шутливые фразы + неожиданные длинные скобки со смешными оговорками.
- **Vocabulary:** Tasty, good, pure, fruit, us, you, people, planet, hello, little (как в «little tasty drink»).
- **Examples:** «We promise we only ever use wonky fruit.» / «Help us help the planet while drinking nice stuff.» / «Tastes like summer, feels like a hug.»
