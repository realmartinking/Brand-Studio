## Mailchimp

Email marketing platform founded in 2001, acquired by Intuit for $12 billion in 2021. Freddie the Chimp is one of the most recognisable brand mascots in SaaS. Mailchimp made small business email marketing feel like something a human designed for humans — in a category that had previously felt like enterprise software.

## Why this position

All-few 0.2: Built explicitly for small businesses, freelancers, and individuals — the people that enterprise software ignores. Free plan up to 500 contacts. The onboarding celebrates first campaigns.

Serious-playful 0.8: The most playful brand in enterprise-adjacent software. Freddie gives a thumbs up when you send your first campaign. Error messages have personality. The UI has jokes that you find only after months of use. Humor is a brand value, not a marketing tactic.

Distance 0.2: Mailchimp speaks in first person, second person, and uses your name. The voice is "the smart friend who works in marketing" — not a software company.

Temperature 0.3: Warm — the yellow brand color, Freddie's golden fur, the rounded illustration style. The brand looks like it was drawn by a person.

Repeatability 0.1: Email is one of the most routine activities in business. Mailchimp's ritual is weekly or daily — maximum routine.

Participation 0.6: The user creates all the content. Mailchimp is a canvas, not a product. The user's audience is the community that forms around the brand.

Body-mind 0.6: More mind than body — the product is ideas made transmissible. But the brand itself is warm and physical in its illustration style.

Speed 0.2: Send now. The fundamental action is immediate — compose, send, done.

## Attractor difference

Friendly-helper attractor: all=0.2, playful=0.7, intimate=0.2, fast=0.3, warm=0.4. Body_mind at 0.6 vs 0.5 reflects that Mailchimp is genuinely a tool that amplifies human ideas, giving it a slight mind-orientation above the neutral attractor position.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Cooper Light (сериф с характером) — нестандартный выбор для tech-бренда, делающий его тёплым. Дополнение — Helvetica Neue в теле. Freddie (обезьяна) как иконический элемент.
- **Palette:** Жёлтый «Cavendish Yellow» (#FFE01B) — главный. Чёрный. Белый. Изредка зелёный и синий акценты. Жёлтый = оптимизм = не-корпоративность.
- **Geometry:** Иллюстрация как главный визуальный язык. Причудливые персонажи, монстры, абсурдные сцены. Ничего технологически холодного.
- **Density:** Низкая-средняя. Много иллюстраций, воздух, одна история на экране.
- **Textures:** Иллюстрированные текстуры — шерсть, бумага, краска. Цифровые но тёплые.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Товарищ-помощник. Разговорный, с самоиронией. Никогда не использует корпоративный жаргон.
- **Phrase length:** Короткие дружелюбные. Часто разговорные конструкции.
- **Vocabulary:** Send, grow, audience, email, campaign, small business, people, real, together, easy.
- **Examples:** «Send better email.» / «All the marketing tools you need, in one place.» / «We think small businesses deserve big dreams.»
