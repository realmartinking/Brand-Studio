## McDonald's

40,000 locations in 100+ countries. 69 million customers per day. The Golden Arches are more recognizable globally than the Christian cross (the statistic that McDonald's never quotes). Ronald McDonald is the second most recognizable fictional character after Santa Claus. McDonald's is not a restaurant brand — it is a cultural infrastructure.

## Why this position

All-few 0.05: The closest to absolute democratic access in this dataset. Price, location density, and menu accessibility make McDonald's available to virtually everyone in any market it enters. Value menu as brand commitment.

Loud-quiet 0.05: The loudest visual brand in this dataset — the Golden Arches are designed to be visible from a moving vehicle at 60mph. Red and yellow are the highest-attention color combination in the visible spectrum (also used in emergency services). This is not accidental.

Speed 0.1 / Rhythm 0.1: Maximum speed. Drive-through as innovation — the entire value proposition is time. "Fast food" is the category definition.

Distance 0.1: First-name basis globally. "Can I get you anything else?" is a hospitality question delivered at industrial scale. The staff are trained to be warm. The PlayPlace is proximity.

Control-release 0.9: The most controlled consumer brand in existence. Every French fry thickness is specified globally. The Hamburger University (established 1961) trains thousands annually. The QSCV system (Quality, Service, Cleanliness, Value) is measured at every location worldwide.

Community 0.7: Family is the structural consumer unit. "I'm Lovin' It" campaigns feature groups. The Happy Meal is designed for intergenerational use. Community building is explicit strategy.

Temperature 0.2: Maximum warmth — yellow + red = thermal color. McDonald's interiors are literally warm colors designed to increase appetite and reduce dwell time.

Repeatability 0.1: The most routine purchase in this dataset. Weekly or daily. The brand has been built to be habit — consistency is the habit loop trigger.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Lovin' Sans — фирменный шрифт McDonald's, 2017. Округлый, тёплый, адаптированный для всех размеров. До — Helvetica.
- **Palette:** Красный McDonald's + жёлтый Golden Arches — самая узнаваемая пара в мире. В премиальных суббрендах (McCafé) добавляется коричневый и кремовый.
- **Geometry:** Золотые арки М — идеальная форма, которая одновременно буква и архитектура. Все элементы системы идут от этой формы.
- **Density:** Очень низкая. Один продукт, один акцент, один фон. Скорость как принцип визуальной коммуникации.
- **Textures:** Бумажные обёртки, картон упаковки, глянец рекламы. Трение быта.

## Verbal code
- **Tone:** Всеобщий, инклюзивный, локализованный под каждую страну. Говорит на языке каждого — не навязывает стиль жизни, а отражает существующий.
- **Phrase length:** Максимально короткие. Три-четыре слова. Слоган — четыре слоги.
- **Vocabulary:** Love, you, feel, taste, together, always, every, I'm lovin' it.
