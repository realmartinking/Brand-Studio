## Muji

"Mujirushi Ryohin" — "no-brand quality goods." Founded in 1980 as a rejection of premium branding in Japanese retail. The name is the concept. Every product is identified only by its function. The brand is the absence of brand.

## Why this position

Control-release 0.9: The system is rigid and complete — packaging format, color palette (natural/beige/white), typography (Gill Sans on Japanese forms), product taxonomy. The control is what allows the no-brand concept to hold.

All-few 0.2: Deliberately democratic. Prices are moderate. Categories span toothbrushes to furniture to houses. The idea is that anyone should be able to afford good design.

Loud-quiet 0.95: The quietest brand in retail. No logo on products. The packaging label is a minimum. The store is organized by category, not display. White fixtures throughout.

Roots-horizon 0.3: Rooted in Japanese aesthetic tradition — wabi-sabi, functional beauty, material honesty. Not forward-looking — Muji's proposition is that good design doesn't need to be new.

Nature-technology 0.3: Natural materials emphasized — unbleached cotton, unprocessed wood, recycled paper. The processing shows. The material IS the aesthetic.

Contrast 0.9: Near-zero contrast within the system. No color clash. Palette of natural tones only — the only drama is material texture.

Repeatability 0.2: Products are for daily use — the razor, the notebook, the humidifier. Routine is the entire point.

Geometry 0.5: Neither angular nor rounded — Muji occupies the functional middle. Rectangles are rectangles. Circles are circles. No styling.

## Attractor difference

Nordic-purity is the closest attractor — shared values: democratic access, extreme restraint, natural materials, low contrast. The key difference: Nordic-purity attractor has a warmer and more lifestyle-editorial quality (Kinfolk, HAY — products that exist to be photographed). Muji refuses even that aspiration. Its quiet is more radical and more functional than Nordic-purity — the no-brand position is a philosophical stance, not an aesthetic choice. Also: roots=0.3 vs attractor's 0.4 — Muji is more historically grounded in pre-modern Japanese values.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Helvetica Neue или собственный нейтральный гротеск. Никогда — декоративный. Текст — информация, не украшение.
- **Palette:** Молочный, бежевый, серый, натуральный крафт. Нет фирменного «цвета бренда» — принципиально. Цвет = материал.
- **Geometry:** Прямоугольник и квадрат как единственные формы. Никаких кривых кроме утилитарно необходимых.
- **Density:** Очень низкая. Один предмет на белом фоне. Каталог — белая тишина с предметами.
- **Textures:** Крафт-бумага упаковки, необработанный лён, полипропилен прозрачный, стали матовая. Текстура материала = весь дизайн.

## Verbal code
