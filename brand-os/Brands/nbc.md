## NBC

National Broadcasting Company, founded 1926, owned by NBCUniversal (Comcast). America's oldest major broadcast network. The peacock logo — originally created to promote color television in 1956, redesigned by Chermayeff & Geismar in 1986 into the iconic six-feather multicolor form — is one of the most recognized broadcast marks in the world. Home of "Must See TV" (1990s Thursday lineup: Seinfeld, Friends, ER), The Tonight Show, Super Bowl broadcasts, and Olympic Games. The peacock symbol was revived for the streaming service Peacock in 2020.
