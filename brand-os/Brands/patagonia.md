## Patagonia

Founded by Yvon Chouinard as a climbing equipment company in 1973. In 2022, Chouinard transferred ownership to a trust and NGO to fight climate change. The brand's most famous ad: "Don't Buy This Jacket" (published in the New York Times on Black Friday). Patagonia is the brand that turned refusal into marketing.

## Why this position

Self-community 0.7: The customer is part of a movement. Patagonia builds identity around shared values — environmental activism, repair culture, anti-consumption. The community is not a marketing device — it is the structural fact of the brand.

Nature-technology 0.7: The product uses technical fabrics (Gore-Tex, Yulex natural rubber) but the brand is coded as nature-aligned. The tension is honest — high-tech materials in service of being in the natural world.

All-few 0.3: Patagonia prices are premium but the brand values democratic access — it is not exclusivity by price point that defines it. It is self-selection by values. You have to care about the right things to be a Patagonia customer.

Loud-quiet 0.4: Relatively quiet branding but the political voice is loud — campaigns against government land seizures, anti-consumption advertising, funding for activists. The brand is loud in values, quiet in visual register.

Participation 0.7: Worn Wear program (repair your gear), Ironclad Guarantee, customer repair workshops. The customer is actively involved in the lifecycle of the product. This is genuine co-creation of the brand's meaning.

Temperature 0.3: Warm — the community feel, the outdoor photography, the earth tones in product. Not a cool brand aesthetically.

Distance 0.2: Deeply intimate. Yvon Chouinard wrote the company's manifesto as a book ("Let My People Go Surfing") available to any customer. The founding story is the brand.

Repeatability 0.3: Products are meant to last for decades. The brand promotes repair over repurchase — unusual repeat-purchase model.

## Visual code
- **Typography:** Freight Text Pro (editorial/body) + Houschka Alt (display) — сочетание журналистской серьёзности с активной энергией. Функциональный, никогда вычурный.
- **Palette:** Stone grey, teal, coral, earthy brown, alpine yellow. Палитра цветов горного снаряжения — яркие из соображений безопасности, нейтральные из уважения к природе.
- **Geometry:** Органические формы горного рельефа. Силуэты гор как базовый визуальный мотив. Логотип — горный хребет.
- **Density:** Средняя-высокая. Длинные нарративные материалы о защите природы + технические детали продукта.
- **Textures:** Флис (изобрели в партнёрстве с Malden Mills), регенерированный нейлон, лоскутные вставки ремонта, потёртые швы после экспедиции.
