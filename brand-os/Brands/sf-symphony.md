## San Francisco Symphony

The San Francisco Symphony is a professional orchestra founded in 1911, based at Davies Symphony Hall in San Francisco. Under Music Director Esa-Pekka Salonen (appointed 2020), the SFS has pursued an agenda of musical innovation, commissioning new works and reimagining the classical concert experience. Previously under Michael Tilson Thomas (MTT) for 25 years, the orchestra achieved international recording recognition. COLLINS redesigned the SFS visual identity to reflect the organization's dual commitment to canonical repertoire and contemporary experimentation — a typographic system bold enough to compete with commercial entertainment while maintaining institutional authority.
