## The North Face

Founded 1966 in San Francisco, named for the coldest and most challenging side of a mountain. Originally a climbing and hiking equipment supplier. Became the defining outdoor-to-streetwear crossover brand — the Nuptse jacket on a Manhattan commuter is as recognizable as the same jacket on a Himalayan expedition.

## Why this position

Body-mind 0.9: The brand's fundamental promise is physical — performance for bodies in demanding conditions. Every product specification is expressed in physical terms: waterproof rating, insulation fill power, weight in grams. The body is the primary.

Serious-playful 0.4: Serious about performance and safety — these products are used in genuinely dangerous conditions. But there is joy in the physical (the summit, the descent, the movement). Not playful but not grim.

Nature-technology 0.6: Nature is the context but technology is the material answer. Gore-Tex, ThermoBall, FutureLight — proprietary technologies are core brand claims. The goal is to perform in nature using engineered materials.

Loud-quiet 0.3: The half-dome logo is prominent and recognisable. Products are color-coded for visibility (safety in mountain environments). Louder than a quiet brand but not urban-energy loud.

Community 0.4: Genuine community — the mountaineering and outdoor world is a real community of practice. Expeditions, athlete ambassadors, guided programs. The community is functional (people who need the same information) not just cultural.

Participation 0.6: The brand enables participation in the physical world — you go to the mountain, you do the thing. The experience the brand enables is the co-created value. Repair and warranty programs extend this.

Repeatability 0.5: The brand occupies both registers — the expedition jacket is an event purchase, the daily commuter puffer is routine. Unusual split across this axis.

Weight 0.3: Gear should be heavy-duty and protective. Lightness is an engineering goal (pack weight) but the product has to be sturdy.

## Visual code

- **Typography:** Bold condensed sans — функциональный и прямой. Логотип: полудом в круге, «THE NORTH FACE» заглавными. Читаем с 20 метров — это экипировочная коммуникация.
- **Palette:** Чёрный, красный, жёлтый — три функциональных цвета видимости на горе. Сезонные палитры шире, но core identity — тёмный фон с яркими акцентами.
- **Geometry:** Угловатые функциональные формы. Нет декоративных линий. Силуэт определяется функцией — утепление, защита, движение.
- **Density:** Средняя. Технические характеристики на упаковке важны (waterproof rating, fill power). Streetwear-продукт коммуницирует проще.
- **Textures:** Gore-Tex мембрана, рипстоп нейлон, синтетический утеплитель. Материал воспринимается через звук и тактильность — шелест, жёсткость, лёгкость.
