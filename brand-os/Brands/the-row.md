## The Row

Founded by Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen in 2006. Named for Savile Row — the tailoring standard, not the location. The brand produces no advertising campaigns. Shows are private and press is minimal. A cashmere coat costs $5,000. It is considered the most critically respected American luxury fashion house currently operating.

## Why this position

Loud-quiet 0.95: The quietest possible position for a fashion brand. No logo. No branding on garments. The fabric quality is the only signal. The Olsens do not give interviews and do not appear in their own imagery.

All-few 0.9: Ultra-exclusive pricing and distribution. Limited stockists. No e-commerce until recently and even then, deliberately minimal. The brand selects its customers by refusing to pursue them.

Serious-playful 0.8: Completely serious. There is no irony, no seasonal theme, no conceptual "world." The Row is the opposite of fashion as entertainment — it treats clothing as architecture.

Self-community 0.2: The most individual position in this dataset for fashion. This is not a community brand — there is no lifestyle to buy into, no tribe to join. The garment is between the maker and the wearer.

Nature-technology 0.2: Cashmere, silk, cotton, leather. The material list reads like a farming catalogue. No synthetic fibers, no technical performance fabrics.

Rhythm 0.9 / Speed 0.7: Deliberately slow production, deliberate purchase pace. The brand does not participate in the seasonal urgency of fashion. Collections arrive when ready.

Participation 0.1: The most passive purchase in fashion — you receive the object. It is complete. There is no co-creation, no customization, no story to complete.

## Visual code

- **Typography:** Минималистичный sans-serif с широким трекингом — нейтральный до невидимости. «the row» строчными. Нет декоративных шрифтов никогда.
- **Palette:** Слоновая кость, кремовый, молочный белый, угольный серый, чёрный. Ни одного цвета. Палитра определяется тканью, не графическим решением.
- **Geometry:** Архитектурные прямоугольные силуэты. Крой — точный, без лишнего. Форма как структура.
- **Density:** Экстремально низкая. Один предмет. Один образ. Никаких editorial-нарративов.
- **Textures:** Двойной кашемир, тяжёлый шёлк-крепе, кожа vegetable-tanned. Ткань — весь дизайн.
