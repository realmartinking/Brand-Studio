## Tribeca Film Festival

Tribeca Film Festival was founded in 2002 by Robert De Niro, Jane Rosenthal, and Craig Hatkoff as a response to the September 11 attacks — to revitalize Lower Manhattan and celebrate the resilience of New York City. Held annually in April/May across venues in the Tribeca neighborhood and broader Manhattan, the festival focuses on independent cinema, documentary, and emerging voices, while also programing narrative features, episodic content, and immersive experiences. COLLINS designed the festival's visual identity to capture the energy of New York independent film culture: bold, typographically driven, and distinctly urban without being generic.
